Complete Stage 2 Syllabus

📊 ERD Modeling

Entities, Attributes, Relationships (1:1, 1:M, M:N), Association Tables, and Crow's Foot notation mapping.

🔑 Key Architecture

Primary, Foreign, Composite, Candidate, Alternate Keys. Deep dive into Surrogate (UUIDv7/Snowflake) vs Natural Keys.

🛡️ Constraints & Integrity

NOT NULL, UNIQUE, CHECK, DEFAULT. Referential actions: CASCADE, RESTRICT, SET NULL, NO ACTION.

🧼 Normalization (1NF - 5NF)

Eliminating insertion, deletion, and update anomalies. Step-by-step normal form decomposition up to BCNF and 4NF/5NF.

⚡ Denormalization & OLAP

Strategic redundancy for read-heavy scale. Star vs Snowflake schema design for data warehouses.

🚀 Migrations & Lifecycle

Up/Down migration scripts, blue-green deployment schemas, rollback safety, and idempotent seeding.
